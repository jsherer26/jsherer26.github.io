What is out club about?

The University of South Carolina Cybersecurity Club is a student led organization for anyone interested in learning cybersecurity knowledge and skills. Our purpose is to promote the learning of various cyber and information security principles through education and hands on practice. We welcome students of any skill level and they are welcome to join our discord to get more information including meeting times and announcements.
